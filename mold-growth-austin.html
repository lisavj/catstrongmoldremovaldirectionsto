Can mold come back after remediation?

Mold can return if the underlying moisture problem isn't resolved. Continuous monitoring and prevention are key.

Can I do mold remediation myself?

While small areas can be treated by homeowners, professional remediation is recommended for larger infestations to ensure complete and safe removal.

Should I test for mold before buying a new house?

Yes, it's wise to test for mold before purchasing a property to avoid future health and financial problems.
