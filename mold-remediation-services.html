Frequently Asked Questions

Is mold inspection necessary after water damage?

Yes, it's crucial to inspect for mold after water damage to prevent potential mold growth.

What are the signs that I might have mold in my house?

Common signs include musty odors, visible mold growth, water leaks, and health symptoms like allergies or respiratory issues.

What is involved in professional mold remediation?

Professional remediation includes assessment, containment, air filtration, mold removal, cleaning, and restoration.
