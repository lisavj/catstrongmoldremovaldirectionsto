Frequently Asked Questions

How can I prevent mold growth in my home?

Prevent mold by controlling humidity levels, ensuring good ventilation, fixing leaks, and keeping the area dry.

Are there any legal implications of mold in rental properties?

Landlords are generally required to ensure their properties are mold-free and safe for tenants, failing which can lead to legal consequences.

Can mold come back after remediation?

Mold can return if the underlying moisture problem isn't resolved. Continuous monitoring and prevention are key.
