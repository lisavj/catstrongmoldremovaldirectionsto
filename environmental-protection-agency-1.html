Frequently Asked Questions

What are the costs associated with mold inspection and remediation?

Costs vary depending on the size of the area and the severity of the mold problem. Inspections can range from $200 to $600, while remediation can cost from $500 to several thousand dollars.

Is mold inspection necessary after water damage?

Yes, it's crucial to inspect for mold after water damage to prevent potential mold growth.

Can I do mold remediation myself?

While small areas can be treated by homeowners, professional remediation is recommended for larger infestations to ensure complete and safe removal.
