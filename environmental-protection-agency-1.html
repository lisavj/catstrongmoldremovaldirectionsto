Frequently Asked Questions

What health issues can mold cause?

Mold can cause allergies, respiratory problems, and other health issues, especially in sensitive individuals.

Are there any legal implications of mold in rental properties?

Landlords are generally required to ensure their properties are mold-free and safe for tenants, failing which can lead to legal consequences.

Can mold affect the value of my property?

Yes, untreated mold can significantly reduce the value of your property and make it harder to sell.
