Frequently Asked Questions

How can I prevent mold growth in my home?

Prevent mold by controlling humidity levels, ensuring good ventilation, fixing leaks, and keeping the area dry.

What should I do if I find mold in my home?

If you find mold, assess the size of the area. If it's small, you might clean it yourself. For larger areas, contact a professional.

What is involved in professional mold remediation?

Professional remediation includes assessment, containment, air filtration, mold removal, cleaning, and restoration.
