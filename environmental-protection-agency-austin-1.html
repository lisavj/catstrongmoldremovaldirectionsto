Is mold inspection necessary after water damage?

Yes, it's crucial to inspect for mold after water damage to prevent potential mold growth.

What should I do if I find mold in my home?

If you find mold, assess the size of the area. If it's small, you might clean it yourself. For larger areas, contact a professional.

How often should I get a mold inspection?

I recommend getting a mold inspection at least once a year, or if you notice signs of mold or water damage.
