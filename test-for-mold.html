Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I find mold in my home?

If you find mold, assess the size of the area. If it's small, you might clean it yourself. For larger areas, contact a professional.

What is mold testing and how is it done?

Mold testing involves sampling the air or surfaces to identify the type and concentration of mold spores present.

Can mold affect the value of my property?

Yes, untreated mold can significantly reduce the value of your property and make it harder to sell.
