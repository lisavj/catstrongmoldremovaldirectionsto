Frequently Asked Questions

Are there different types of mold, and are some more harmful than others?

Yes, there are many types of mold. Some, like black mold, can be more harmful and should be addressed immediately.

How is mold remediation different from mold removal?

Mold removal focuses on eliminating mold, while remediation addresses the root cause and prevents future growth.

What are the costs associated with mold inspection and remediation?

Costs vary depending on the size of the area and the severity of the mold problem. Inspections can range from $200 to $600, while remediation can cost from $500 to several thousand dollars.
