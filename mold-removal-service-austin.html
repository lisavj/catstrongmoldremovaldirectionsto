    How often should I get a mold inspection?

    I recommend getting a mold inspection at least once a year, or if you notice signs of mold or water damage.

    Can mold come back after remediation?

    Mold can return if the underlying moisture problem isn't resolved. Continuous monitoring and prevention are key.

    What is a mold inspection and why do I need one?

    A mold inspection involves examining your property for signs of mold growth. It's important for identifying potential health hazards and preventing structural damage.
